Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become Tunisia's premier glass decoration and personalization brand — setting the standard for craftsmanship, design, and industrial quality across North Africa and the MENA region.

We envision a future where "decorated in Tunisia" is synonymous with premium quality, where every glass surface tells a story crafted by expert hands and designed by creative minds.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional glass decoration solutions that elevate brands, celebrate moments, and set new benchmarks for the industry — by combining artisan expertise with commercial excellence.

Through the DecoLux alliance, we bridge the gap between traditional glass craftsmanship and modern market demands, creating value for our partners, clients, and the Tunisian economy.
