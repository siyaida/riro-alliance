Legal Framework

SARL Structure

Company Formation

RIRO SARL — Société à Responsabilité Limitée registered under Tunisian law (Code de Commerce).

  • Ownership: 50% Ridha (Bel Décor) / 50% Anthony (MEZZO GRP)
  • Management: Co-gérance (co-management)
  • Registered: Tunisia
  • Activity: Glass decoration, personalization & packaging

Asset Structure

Assets are leased to the company — not contributed as capital. This protects both partners' personal property.

  • Machines: Ridha's property, leased to RIRO
  • Premises: Anthony's property, leased to RIRO
  • Lease payments: Compte courant associé (shareholder loans)
  • On dissolution: Assets return to original owners

Management Team

Ridha — Production Director

  • Client relationship management

Comprehensive business review. Financial statements, market analysis, strategic adjustments, and partner satisfaction assessment.
