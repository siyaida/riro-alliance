Overview

Executive Summary

DecoLux is a strategic business alliance between Bel Décor (glass decoration production) and MEZZO GRP (commercial design & sales), formed as an SARL under Tunisian law. The alliance targets Tunisia's underserved glass decoration market across four segments: personalized objects, grande distribution, cosmetics, and olive oil packaging.

With an initial investment of just 22,000 TND, production capacity of 2,000 pieces/day, and break-even at 8,000 pieces/month, DecoLux offers a low-risk, high-upside opportunity with projected ROI recovery within 2 months of reaching operational maturity.

Market Analysis

The Opportunity

🇯🇳

Tunisian Market

Growing domestic demand for premium glass products across hospitality, retail, and events sectors. No dominant structured player in decoration.

🍂

Olive Oil Export

Tunisia is the world's 2nd largest olive oil exporter. Premium bottle decoration adds significant export value and brand differentiation.

💎

Cosmetics Growth

North African beauty market expanding rapidly. Local and international brands seek premium glass packaging manufactured locally.

🎁

Personalization Trend

Growing culture of personalized gifts for weddings, corporate events, and special occasions — high margins and repeat business.

Looking Ahead

Growth Strategy

Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1–3)

Company registration, production setup, first 5 clients activated, showroom opening. Target: 5K-10K TND/month.

Phase 2: Market Penetration (Month 3–6)

10+ active clients, break-even achieved, product portfolio refined, quality standards established. Target: 15K-20K TND/month.

Phase 3: Operational Maturity (Month 6–12)

Full capacity production, 20+ clients, cosmetics and olive oil segments activated, brand recognition building. Target: 25K+ TND/month.

Phase 4: Scale (Year 2)

New product lines, additional equipment, potential export market entry, 40K+ TND/month revenue.

Phase 5: Expansion (Year 3-5)

Regional expansion, possible second production line, brand licensing opportunities, strategic partnerships with international brands.
