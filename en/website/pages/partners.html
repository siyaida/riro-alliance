Ridha

Bel Décor — Production Director

Background & Expertise

With over 15 years of dedicated experience in glass decoration, Ridha has built Bel Décor into a recognized name in Tunisian glass craftsmanship. His workshop has served diverse clients from individual customers to industrial accounts, developing proprietary techniques that deliver consistent quality at scale.

Contributions to DecoLux

  • Machines & Equipment: ~50,000 TND in specialized glass decoration machinery (leased to company)
  • Technical Expertise: 15+ years of glass decoration knowledge, techniques, and process optimization
  • Client Portfolio: Established relationships with existing customers across multiple segments
  • Production Management: Ability to manage 2,000 pieces/day output with quality assurance
  • Workforce Training: Capability to train and develop production teams from scratch

Role in the Alliance

As Production Director, Ridha oversees all manufacturing operations, quality control, production scheduling, equipment maintenance, and team development. He is the operational core of DecoLux — indispensable to delivery and quality.

Anthony

MEZZO GRP — Commercial Director

Background & Expertise

Anthony brings commercial acumen and premium design sensibility through MEZZO GRP. His experience spans business development, brand positioning, and creating market-ready products that command premium pricing. His network and showroom infrastructure provide the commercial backbone DecoLux needs.

Contributions to DecoLux

  • Premises & Showroom: Physical space for operations and client-facing presentations (leased to company)
  • Investment Capital: ~22,000 TND initial investment for operational setup and working capital
  • Premium Design: Design capabilities that transform products into premium market offerings
  • Commercial Network: Business relationships and market access for sales growth
  • Business Development: Strategic planning and market expansion expertise

Role in the Alliance

As Commercial Director, Anthony drives sales strategy, manages the showroom experience, leads design direction, develops business partnerships, and handles marketing and brand positioning. He is the market-facing engine that converts production into revenue.
