DecoLux BUSINESS ALLIANCE

Executive Summary

Bel Décor × MEZZO GRP — Redefining Glass Decoration in Tunisia

Confidential — March 2026

The Opportunity

Tunisia's glass decoration and personalization market is experiencing growing demand across multiple sectors — personalized gifts, cosmetics packaging, olive oil branding, and grande distribution. Yet the market lacks a structured, quality-driven player capable of delivering premium glass decoration at industrial scale. DecoLux fills that gap.

By combining Bel Décor's 15+ years of glass decoration mastery with MEZZO GRP's commercial infrastructure and premium design capabilities, DecoLux is positioned to become Tunisia's premier glass decoration and personalization brand.

Why This Works

Perfect Complementarity: Ridha cannot scale without commercial infrastructure. Anthony cannot produce without manufacturing expertise. Neither succeeds alone — together, they cover the entire value chain.

Challenge (Alone) Solution (Together)
Ridha lacks showroom & design Anthony provides both
Anthony lacks production capability Ridha delivers 2,000 pieces/day
Neither has brand positioning DecoLux creates a unified premium brand
Limited market reach Combined network doubles reach

Asset Protection: Machines and premises remain personal property (leased to company). If the alliance ends, each partner retains their assets.

Low Risk, High Upside: Only 22,000 TND investment with break-even at 8,000 pieces/month — achievable within the first quarter. Downside is limited; upside is a scalable industrial business.
