Risk Mitigation

Managing Key Risks

⚖️

50/50 Deadlock

Equal ownership can block decisions when partners disagree

→ Mediation clause + casting vote protocol on predefined topics

📉

Dilution Risk

Future investment rounds could shift ownership balance

→ Anti-dilution clauses + pre-emption rights in shareholder agreement

🔐

Control Loss

Commercial partner could dominate client relationships

→ Defined domains + client relationships as company asset + non-compete
